FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Company registration involves: 1) Obtaining Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) 2) Applying for Director Identification Number (DIN) 3) Name approval from MCA 4) Filing incorporation documents 5) Receiving Certificate of Incorporation. The entire process takes 7-15 days with our expert assistance.
GST registration with DNT starts at ₹1,999/- which includes complete documentation support, ARN generation within 3-5 days, and free consultation. Government fees are included in this price.
GST registration typically takes 3-7 working days. With proper documentation, you can receive your ARN (Application Reference Number) within 3 days and GSTIN within 7 days of application.
GST registration is mandatory if your annual turnover exceeds ₹40 lakhs (₹20 lakhs for special category states) for goods, or ₹20 lakhs for services. E-commerce sellers and inter-state suppliers need mandatory registration regardless of turnover.
For Private Limited Company registration, you need: 1) PAN & Aadhaar of directors 2) Passport-size photos 3) Address proof (Electricity bill/Bank statement) 4) Registered office proof (Rent agreement/NOC) 5) Digital Signature Certificate. We guide you through the entire documentation process.
