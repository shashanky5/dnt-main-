ITR is Mandatory For...

Understanding your filing obligations helps you avoid severe late-payment penalties.

Employees

Mandatory for resident individuals earning above ₹2.5L-₹5L (based on age).

Businesses

Proprietors, partners, and corporations must file regardless of profit or loss.

Investors

Crucial for those earning from stocks, mutual funds, or real estate capital gains.

NRIs & Global

Individuals with foreign assets or income earned in India above thresholds.
